Daria Shymanska

Contemporary dancer and choreographer

Hello, I am Daria! A professional dancer since 2013, I have been trining both kids and adults in such styles as contemporary and modern dance. My focus is to help my clients not only memorize and perfect choreography pieces but to discover their bodies, what they are capable of and how to interact with it. With kids, we combine physical exercises with actual choreography to help them built healthy relations with their bodies from the very beginning. Moreover, I offer separate stretching classesfor everyone who is not ready to dance yet but wants to keep their bodies in shape and feel fit.
You can choose a group class or an individual lesson both offline and online.
Please contact me for additional info!
